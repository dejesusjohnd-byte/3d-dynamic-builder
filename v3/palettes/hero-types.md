## A — Full-Bleed Cinematic
Video fills 100vw x 100vh. Minimal text visible on load.
Content reveals as user scrolls into the pinned hero.
CTA appears at ~40% scroll progress.
**Use when:** wide camera framing, cinematic/immersive mood, premium brand.
**Avoid when:** urgent action needed immediately, information-heavy site.
**Nav:** ghost minimal or hidden.

## B — Split Tension
60/40 or 50/50 vertical split. Video one side, bold headline the other.
Hard visual divide (line or gap). CTA in text column.
**Use when:** product-focused, direct/confident adjectives, medium camera framing.
**Avoid when:** abstract video, emotional storytelling brand.
**Nav:** bold persistent or ghost minimal.

## C — Texture Type
Video runs at 20–40% opacity as background texture.
Enormous display type (96px+) dominates — almost no other elements above fold.
**Use when:** close camera framing, raw/industrial/editorial mood, type-forward brand.
**Avoid when:** warm/inviting brand, trust-heavy industry, weak typography.
**Nav:** ghost minimal or side vertical.

## D — Bottom-Anchored Statement
Full-bleed video fills viewport. All text anchored to bottom edge.
Top 70% is pure video. Brand statement sits at bottom like a film title card.
**Use when:** landscape/wide video, expansive mood, nature/environment brands.
**Avoid when:** mobile-first critical, multiple CTAs needed above fold.
**Nav:** ghost minimal (very light, doesn't compete with video).

## E — Asymmetric Grid
CSS grid hero — unequal columns. Video in one grid cell, not full-bleed.
Text and other elements intentionally break grid alignment.
**Use when:** editorial/creative brand, portfolio, agency.
**Avoid when:** corporate/formal, mass market, trust-first industries.
**Nav:** side vertical or hidden reveal.

## F — Minimal Dark Ambient
Dark background. Video plays as subtle ambient light source (low opacity, blurred).
One headline. One CTA. Maximum negative space. Nothing competes.
**Use when:** luxury/premium, mysterious/dark mood, tech or exclusive products.
**Avoid when:** warm/community brands, family-oriented, high information density.
**Nav:** ghost minimal or hidden reveal.
