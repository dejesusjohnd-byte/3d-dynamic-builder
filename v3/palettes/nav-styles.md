# Palette: Nav Styles
Select ONE per build. Log the ID in PALETTE_BLOCK.

---

## A — Ghost Minimal
Transparent background on load. Thin blur-glass on scroll.
4 or fewer nav items. Logo left, links right or center. No border until scroll.
**Use when:** immersive hero (A, D, F), premium/cinematic brand.
**Avoid when:** many pages, utility-heavy site, complex navigation needs.

## B — Side Vertical Fixed
Navigation runs vertically along the left or right edge. Fixed position.
Items listed top-to-bottom. Unusual — high distinctiveness.
**Use when:** portfolio, single-scroll storytelling, editorial brand (E hero).
**Avoid when:** e-commerce with many pages, mobile-first priority.

## C — Bold Persistent + CTA
Full-width bar visible at all times. Logo left, items center, CTA button right.
Never hides. Slight background always present.
**Use when:** lead generation, booking, direct-response — primary action is urgent.
**Avoid when:** immersive/cinematic hero, portfolio, storytelling-first brand.

## D — Hidden Reveal (Scroll Up)
Nav hidden on load. Appears only when user scrolls upward — intent signal.
Smooth slide-down transition. Disappears again on scroll down.
**Use when:** immersive long-scroll experience, storytelling site, hero A or D.
**Avoid when:** discovery-dependent navigation, first-time user onboarding critical.

## E — Integrated Hero Nav
Nav is part of the hero section design, not a separate bar.
Could be corner-positioned, overlaid on video, or built into hero layout.
**Use when:** hero type B (split), E (asymmetric), highly custom layouts.
**Avoid when:** multi-page sites needing persistent navigation.
