# Palette: Typography Personalities
Select ONE per build. Log the ID in PALETTE_BLOCK.
All fonts must be loaded from Google Fonts CDN. No system fonts as primary.

---

## T1 — Serif Display + Geometric Sans
Heading: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond (serif, editorial weight)
Body: Inter or DM Sans (geometric, neutral)
Label: Inter (small caps, wide tracking)
Scale ratio: moderate — display ~72px, body 16px
**Use when:** elegant, premium, authoritative, editorial, warm brands.

## T2 — Condensed Heavy + Light Sans
Heading: Bebas Neue or Barlow Condensed Black (condensed, tall, bold)
Body: DM Sans Light or Inter Light
Label: Bebas Neue (uppercase, tracked)
Scale ratio: extreme — display 96–120px, body 14–15px
**Use when:** bold, industrial, raw, energetic, direct brands. Papa's Burger style.

## T3 — Geometric Sans + Mono Labels
Heading: Space Grotesk or Outfit (geometric, slightly techy)
Body: Inter Regular
Label: JetBrains Mono or Space Mono (monospace — signals precision)
Scale ratio: moderate-high — display 64–80px, body 16px
**Use when:** tech, SaaS, cold/precise, systematic brands.

## T4 — Humanist Sans + Serif Body
Heading: Nunito Sans Bold or Plus Jakarta Sans Bold (warm, approachable)
Body: Lora or Source Serif 4 (readable serif for long copy)
Label: Nunito Sans (small, comfortable tracking)
Scale ratio: compressed — display 48–56px, body 18px (legibility-first)
**Use when:** warm, community, service, health, approachable/human brands.

## T5 — Variable Display + Minimal Body
Heading: Cabinet Grotesk ExtraBold or Syne ExtraBold (expressive, contemporary)
Body: Inter or Manrope Light
Label: Syne or Cabinet Grotesk (tracked uppercase)
Scale ratio: extreme with restraint — display 80–100px, body 15px, very few text elements
**Use when:** contemporary creative, portfolio, agencies, brands that lead with personality.
