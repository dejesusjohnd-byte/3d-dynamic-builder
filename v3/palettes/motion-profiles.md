# Palette: Motion Profiles
Select ONE per build. Log the ID in PALETTE_BLOCK.
Profile sets timing, easing, and stagger for ALL animations on the page.

---

## M1 — Slow Luxury
duration: 1.8–2.2s | ease: power4.out | stagger: 0.15s | scrub: 1
Deliberate, weighty, cinematic. Everything arrives with intention.
Hover: 0.4s ease transitions. No snappy movements.
**Use when:** premium, luxury, editorial, authoritative, trust-heavy brands.
**Avoid when:** tech product, energetic/youthful brand, fast-paced conversion page.

## M2 — Kinetic Modern
duration: 0.6–0.8s | ease: power2.inOut | stagger: 0.05s | scrub: 0.3
Fast, confident, efficient. Motion acknowledges action quickly.
Hover: 0.2s. Snappy but not jarring.
**Use when:** SaaS, tech, startup, data-driven, direct-response brands.
**Avoid when:** luxury, emotional storytelling, brands where calm = trust.

## M3 — Minimalist Restraint
duration: 2.2–2.5s | ease: power1.inOut | stagger: 0.2s | scrub: 1.5
The slowest profile. Maximum breathing room between animations.
Some elements don't animate at all — intentional stillness.
**Use when:** architecture, design studios, ultra-minimalist, editorial brands.
**Avoid when:** energetic, product-heavy, conversion-focused sites.

## M4 — Precision Utility
duration: 0.4–0.5s | ease: power2.out | stagger: 0.04s | scrub: 0.2
Tight, efficient, data-like. Every animation communicates information.
No decorative motion. Animations confirm state changes only.
**Use when:** enterprise, data tools, dashboards, utility products.
**Avoid when:** any brand where feel matters more than function.
