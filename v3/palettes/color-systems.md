# Palette: Color Systems
Select ONE per build. Log the ID in PALETTE_BLOCK.
These are starting approaches — exact hex values come from the video and brief.
Never use pure #000000 or #ffffff. Derive near-blacks and near-whites from the video.

---

## C1 — Dark + Single Accent
bg: near-black derived from video (e.g. #09090b, #0C0C0C, #06080e)
surface: 1–2 steps lighter than bg
text: near-white (e.g. #f4f4f5, #e8e8e8)
accent: ONE strong color from the video (e.g. #c8a97e, #E8291C, #3b82f6)
accent-hover: accent shifted ±15% lightness
**Use when:** cinematic video, dark mood, premium/luxury, immersive experiences.

## C2 — Light + Minimal
bg: near-white (e.g. #fafafa, #f5f5f0, #f8f6f1)
surface: very slightly darker or tinted version of bg
text: near-black (e.g. #111111, #1a1a1a, #0f0f0f)
accent: single dark or muted accent (ink, charcoal, or one saturated tone)
**Use when:** architecture, design studios, editorial, content-first, light minimal brands.

## C3 — Warm Earth
bg: deep warm brown or dark amber (e.g. #1a0f08, #2a1506, #3B1F0E)
surface: mid-warm brown
text: warm cream (e.g. #F5ECD7, #f0e6d0, #e8d8c0)
accent: gold or warm copper (e.g. #c8a97e, #d4a853, #b8860b)
**Use when:** coffee, food, craft, earth/nature brands, warm authoritative brands.

## C4 — Cool Tech
bg: deep navy-black (e.g. #040812, #040d1a, #060e1f)
surface: slightly lighter navy
text: cool off-white (e.g. #e2e8f0, #cdd6e8)
accent: electric blue or cyan (e.g. #3b82f6, #06b6d4, #60a5fa)
muted: blue-grey (e.g. #8899aa)
**Use when:** SaaS, infrastructure, AI, fintech, cybersecurity brands.

## C5 — Editorial Contrast
Choose either:
  Option A: Cream bg (#f8f3ea) + deep red accent (#c0392b or #E8291C) + near-black text
  Option B: Near-black bg + bold warm accent + off-white text (magazine dark mode)
Heavy contrast between text and bg. Accent used sparingly — for emphasis only.
**Use when:** media, publishing, agencies, food brands, bold editorial statement brands.
