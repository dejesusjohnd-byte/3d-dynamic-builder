## AI Video Generation Prompt

A multi-angle sequential food commercial of a gourmet burger deconstructing in extreme slow-motion.

**Sequence 1 — Low-angle tracking (EXTREME SLOW-MOTION)**
Low-angle dramatic tracking shot. Tilt-up from burger on dark rustic table. Burger explodes vertically. Camera slowly dollys back and pushes up. Ends in wide deconstructed view of all ingredients suspended mid-air. Background: dark, volumetric studio lighting with spotlight.
Speed: extreme slow-motion. Camera: moving (tilt + dolly back). Open space: upper corners and flanks beside floating layers.

**Sequence 2 — Overhead static (DYNAMIC BURST)**
Direct overhead top-down camera angle. Burger centered on matte black surface. Camera perfectly locked and static. Burger deconstructs outwards symmetrically — buns, pickles, onions, patty expand outward. Final frame: exploded flat-lay diagram. Bright soft natural lighting.
Speed: dynamic burst (fast). Camera: locked static. Open space: corners (burger expanding toward edges).

**[Hard cut]**

**Sequence 3 — 360° orbital (HIGH ENERGY → DECELERATION)**
Dynamic 360-degree orbital camera circling burger. Burger explodes in slow-motion as camera rotates in sweeping arc. Ultra-detail: moisture beads on lettuce, splashing ketchup droplets, steaming layers. Camera decelerates to stop at three-quarters profile angle. Burger beautifully opened and suspended.
Speed: high-energy then decelerates to still. Camera: moving orbital → stops. Open space: left or right flank depending on orbital position; clear at the 3/4 stop.
