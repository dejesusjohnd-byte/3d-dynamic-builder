## P1 — Mood → Story → Offer → Trust → Close
Hero sets atmosphere. Narrative/collage introduces brand.
Offering grid presents services. Value pause builds trust. CTA closes.
**Use when:** brand storytelling is primary, trust-building industry (consulting, law, finance).
**Section types:** Hero, CollageIntro, OfferingGrid, ValuePause, CTA

## P2 — Impact → Offer → Evidence → Close
Hero leads with the primary action immediately.
Offering/product grid second. Social proof or results third. CTA closes.
**Use when:** direct response, e-commerce, high-urgency conversion goal.
**Section types:** Hero, OfferingGrid, Evidence, CTA

## P3 — Story → Mood → Trust → Offer → Close
Opens with brand narrative. Hero-style visual second (reversed entry).
Trust signals third. Offer fourth. CTA closes.
**Use when:** brand-first strategy, audience already aware of category, portfolio.
**Section types:** NarrativeOpener, MoodVisual, Trust, OfferingGrid, CTA

## P4 — Offer → Story → Proof → Mood → Close
Lead with what you do — no mood-setting preamble.
Brand story second. Social proof third. Atmospheric visual fourth. CTA closes.
**Use when:** product-first brand, audience knows what they want, SaaS/tech.
**Section types:** OfferingHero, Story, Proof, AtmosphericBreak, CTA

## P5 — Mood → Trust → Story → Offer → Close
Hero sets premium atmosphere. Credibility signals immediately after.
Brand narrative third. Offer fourth. CTA closes.
**Use when:** luxury, premium pricing, audience skeptical — trust must come early.
**Section types:** Hero, CredibilityBlock, Story, OfferingGrid, CTA

## P6 — Minimal: Impact → Offer → Close
Three sections only. Hero, what you do, and a closing CTA.
Maximum negative space. No padding sections.
**Use when:** focused landing pages, single-product brands, minimalist brief.
**Section types:** Hero, OfferingBlock, CTA
